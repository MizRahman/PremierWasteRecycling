Collection of cardboard and paper Waste

We collect cardboard and paper Waste from businesses, factories, retail units, wholesalers etc. BD Recycling is registered with the environment agency, and we issue official Duty of Care waste transfer notes. Collections are arranged to suit customers needs and are cost free. Our fleet of vans cover all London areas, Our friendly staff are happy to assist with any enquires.

Baled Waste

Baling waste, we purchase your ready baled paper material, we offer competitive rates, we purchase mill sized bales or any other bale size. We also purchase palleterised grades of paper, in fact we buy any waste paper, get in touch to find out what we can do for you.

Trading of waste paper

We have extensive trade partnerships with mills in China, India, Bangladesh, we offer competitive rates for many grades of waste paper, which we arrange shipping and all documentation with our international partners. All waste paper is turned into pulp and turned back into new recycled paper, saving valuable resources and trees.

We place quality of service very highly on our list of priorities; New Customers are welcomed to view the loading of their material, so the quality can be seen first hand. On average we load 25 MT per 40 ft HC shipping container. Digital images are also provided for each loading, giving customers a real view of their loadings. We can cater for adhoc, and non-common customer requirements and always try to go the extra mile to ensure customer satisfaction.

Baling Wire

Baling Wire, we offer a competitive rate for bale wire, our wire is high grade and suitable for most baling machines, please send us an enquiry if you need any further information.
